Eggnog Latte

Warm up during winter months with a mug of creamy eggnog latte, made with eggnog or Advocaat, milk, espresso and a pinch of nutmeg for dusting

Ingredients

  • 100ml eggnog or Advocaat
  • 100ml milk
  • 50ml espresso, hot and freshly made
  • Small pinch of grated nutmeg

Steps

  1. Gently heat together the milk and eggnog until just simmering (being careful not to boil).
  2. Whisk until frothy or use a milk frother.
  3. Pour the espresso into a large mug or heatproof glass, followed by the eggnog/milk mixture.
  4. Serve dusted with a little nutmeg.
